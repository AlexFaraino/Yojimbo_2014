Hi, I'm Pierre, I'm a front-end developer. I'm currently living in Manly (Australia)

Welcome on my personal website

I've always been interested in website creation. I'm in the front-end world since 2003, for personal projects at the beginning (Gaming portals and forum, flash-based projects …).

After studying networks and telecommunication, I specialized in software development studies, but I was lacking something more design-driven. It's in 2006 that I realized front-end could be my real job.

Since that time, I try my best to follow this fast evolving world, Re-evaluating my knowledge when new ideas arise. Sometimes a bit obsessive, I'm constantly looking for ways to improve my workflow.
